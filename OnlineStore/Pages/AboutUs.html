About Us

Welcome to SYSTEMSTORES, your number one source for all things products. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of products, with a focus on dependability, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2025 by Eric Ekra, SYSTEMSTORES has come a long way from its beginnings in Canada. When Eric first started out, his passion for "eco-friendly products" drove him to do tons of research so that SYSTEMSTORES can offer you the world's most advanced products.

We now serve customers all over Canada, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
